                      Mayo college

    Location: Ajmer, RJ           Type: Boarding               Board: Cbse

Mayo College is a boys-only independent boarding school in Ajmer, Rajasthan, India. Richard Bourke, 6th Earl of Mayo, who was the Viceroy of India from 1869 to 1872, founded it in 1875. This makes it one of the oldest public boarding schools in India.Students aged 9-18 are admitted to this college every year and it imparts world class education based on the curriculum affiliated to CBSE at both HSC and SSC levels.

                   st. joseph's college

    Location: Nainital, UK           Type: Day cum boarding               Board: ICSE

St. Joseph's College Nainital was established in 1888. The site was the location of a seminary, run by the Italian Capuchin Fathers. The seminary later reopened as a Catholic school for boys: St. Joseph's College. The school is still referred to as "SEM" (for Seminary). In 1892 four Christian Brothers took formal charge of St. Joseph's College and thus began the involvement of the Christian Brothers in the running of the school.
